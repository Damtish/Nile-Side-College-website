Admission to Programmes

Admission to the B.Sc. programs at Nile Side College is exclusively available for day-time programs. The B.Sc. program is structured into two categories: the first category caters to individuals with advanced standing, allowing admission for graduates from any field at accredited universities or colleges. Transfer applicants or those from other institutions may request exemptions for relevant courses taken before joining Nile side College. The second category is open to applicants who have successfully passed The Ethiopian Higher Education Entrance Certificate Examination or its equivalent, provided they meet the formal requirements outlined by the Ministry of Science and Higher Education.

Admission Procedure

Admission is open for Ethiopians as well as international students. Apart from the stated requirements for admissions into the different study programmes, Special Criteria for Admission may also be set by the Academic Council when deemed necessary, through entrance exams, interviews or by reviewing merits already attained. The Academic Programme Council determines the number of students who may be admitted to each study programme in each entry according to the collegeâ€™s available resources. The final selection for admission is usually made on a competitive basis and according to the number of places available.
To help the Academic Programme Council assess the applications made for admission to the college, each applicant is required to submit the following to the Admissions Office:

  1. A completed application form obtained from the Office
  2. Grade 12th National Examination Result main and 2 copies
  3. Transcript(9th-12th) the certified must be copies of original documents
  4. Two 2 X 3 size pictures (taken within the last six months)
